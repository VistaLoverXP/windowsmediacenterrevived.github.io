    • What is Media Center?

    The Media Center was a digital video recorder and media player created by Microsoft. It was essentially a way to browse your videos, images, music all on your laptop to your TV!


    Origins of Media Center

    Windows Media Center, orignally came out in 2002, a year after Windows XP. It was planned to be a successor to the cancelled Activity Centers from the Windows Neptune and Windows ME prototype which got constantly removed and replaced for Windows XP
